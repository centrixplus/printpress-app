1

Job Evaluation / Print Cost Estimation Engine

Core Custom Module — Heart of the System

  • Raw Material Costing: Paper type, GSM, size, sheet utilization, waste calculation, cut planning & optimization
  • Printing Specifications: 1 Color, 2 Color, Multi-Color, Recto & Recto-Verso
  • Post-Printing Operations: Die Cutting, Waterbase, Lamination, Spot UV, Hot Foiling, Embossing, Window Patching, Pasting — each with machine, setup, run, consumables & labor costing
  • Machine Selection Engine: Auto-select based on sheet size, print type, color, finishing & quantity
  • Impression-Based Costing: Offset (plate, ink, setup/running/wastage sheets) & Digital (click cost, toner)
  • Waste Calculation Engine: Paper, setup, production & cutting wastage reflected in costing
  • Cost Components: Material, Machine, Labour, Consumables, Operational, Maintenance, Legal & Delivery allocations
  • Profit Margin Engine: Configurable per job, product, customer & category
